# Phoenix — System Language

Each tag has a specific author, specific meaning, specific effect.
Nobody deletes or modifies another agent's entries — everyone only **adds**.

## Evaluation (Curator)

| Tag | Meaning | What follows |
|-----|---------|--------------|
| `Aurum` | Good idea, applicable, advances the solution | System continues |
| `Plumbum` | Bad idea, vague, repetitive | Goes to dead branch |
| `Human` | Curator cannot reliably decide | Human in the loop |

## Alchemist

| Tag | Meaning | What follows |
|-----|---------|--------------|
| `Lux` | Found solution / new angle | Curator evaluates |

## Guardian

| Tag | When | What follows |
|-----|------|--------------|
| `Chaos` | Entropy drops + delta quality ≈ 0 | Alchemist wakes up |
| `Sol` | Entropy drops + quality rises | Exit — solution exists |

## Alchemist (after exhausting attempts)

| Tag | When | What follows |
|-----|------|--------------|
| `Limes` | After 3× Plumbum from Curator | Exit — change problem or model |

## Etymology

- **Aurum / Plumbum** — gold and lead from alchemy. Alchemist transforms Plumbum into Aurum.
- **Lux** — light (Latin). Alchemist illuminated the path.
- **Chaos** — primordial state before order (Greek cosmogony).
- **Sol** — sun (Latin) + solution. End of darkness, answer found.
- **Limes** — border, limit (Latin). Beyond this border lies Wisdom.
- **Human** — where the machine is not enough.
- **Phoenix** — the bird that burns and rises from ash. Transformation through destruction.
